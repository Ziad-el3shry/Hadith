Hadith of the day

Salaf on Nifaq

Al-Bukhari reported: Ibn Abi Mulaykah, may Allah have mercy on him, said, “I met thirty companions of the Prophet, peace and blessings be upon him, and each one of them feared hypocrisy for himself. None of them said they had faith like the Angel Gabriel or the Angel Michael.”

Source: Ṣaḥīḥ al-Bukhārī 1/18
